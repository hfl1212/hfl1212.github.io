Why Pawdy?

Pawdy is a place where students can request pet sitting from other students for free. Students can post their pet sitting needs on Pawdy and others can volunteer to help out fellow students. Pawdy helps build community of trust and fellowship among student pet owners.

Free.

Pawdy is free for all UW students. Users can post and take on pet sitting tasks without charge, ever.

Secure.

Your data is safe with us. We promise to never sell your data. Your privacy and security is our top priority.

Reliable.

We require all UW personnel to sign in with their UW account. You can trust that you will only encounter UW students or staff on our website.

Community-building.

You can build long term relationships with other UW pet owners. We have helped many users foster real friendships and offline play-dates.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Sign up with your UW account. You need to have a valid UW email to use our service.

Post

Post a pet sitting request with pet photos, pet description, and dates pet sitting needed.

Contact

Contact pet owner if you are willing to take on a pet sitting request. Clearly communicate details such as experience and expectations.

About Us

We are a team of 4 undergraduate students at the University of Washington studying Informatics. We are a group of pet-lovers passionate about animal rights. We believe that good and reliable pet care should not be limited to those with financial resources. We commit to using our skills in technology to help all pets receive the care they need.
